About

I am a man of many hobbies...

I love finding new things to dive into. Throughout the years two fields have always stuck with me: I love singing and creating music and I love tech and how its facets have shaped our world. I decided to get a degree in music education and began teaching private lessons out of college.

The pandemic brutally shifted how I had to operate and I ran into many technological barriers to my teaching. I decided to learn JavaScript to build better tools for myself and my students. This reinvigorated my love of tech through coding and pushed me to spend more time on my projects. I have since transitioned to freelancing as a web developer and love to build websites and web apps for clients.

When I'm not building websites you'll find me outside exploring with my wife and dog, playing disc golf, or singing with my friends!
